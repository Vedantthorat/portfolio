Engineering Admission Chatbot

Description: This interactive chatbot assists prospective engineering students with the admission process, offering personalized guidance and essential information.

Key Features:

    Admission Guidance: Detailed insights into first-year engineering admission process.
  • Personalized Responses: College suggestions based on user percentile and past cutoffs.
  • Documentation Assistance: List of necessary documents for a hassle-free admission.

Technologies Used:

  • IBM Technology: Chatbot framework enabling natural language interaction.
  • Data Integration: Cutoff data from previous years for accurate recommendations.

Impact: Simplifies the admission process, empowering students with AI-driven guidance and efficient planning.
